FAQs

What features does this app provide?

Our app allows you to manage your finances efficiently by offering features such as user account management, manual income and expense tracking, goal setting for savings, and detailed statistical analysis of your spending habits.

Is my data secure in this app?

Yes, we prioritize your data security. All information is encrypted and stored securely. We recommend using strong passwords for your account to enhance security.

Can I track multiple income sources and expenses?

Absolutely! You can manually add multiple income sources and categorize your expenses for better financial insights and control.

How can I set financial goals?

Simply navigate to the "Goals" section in the app, input your savings target, and set a deadline. The app will help you monitor your progress towards achieving your goals.
